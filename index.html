The underlying insight driving Predictive Text Labs is that when leaders ask “what should I do?”, they want an actionable answer, not another chart. Anyone who's worked with a CEO knows that actionable answers start with a prediction.

LLMs are powerful; trained on billions of articles, books, and websites, they summarize the past brilliantly. But we still don’t trust them to forecast. That's because reasoning to predict events is fundamentally different from predicting the next token. Real forecasting requires seeing how events cascade, why patterns repeat, and which signals matter.

We believe temporal reasoning isn’t just another AI capability. It’s the core primitive that turns LLMs from search engines into decision-making partners. Without it, AI can analyze the past but never predict the future.

This gap limits every organization trying to leverage AI to drive decisions.

Our mission is to close it.
